**Converse** of a statement **$p \implies q$**: **$q \implies p$**
**sometimes::always/sometimes** true

**Inverse** of a statement **$p \implies q$**: **$\neg p \implies \neg q$**
**sometimes::always/sometimes** true

**Contrapositive** of a statement **$p \implies q$**: **$\neg q \implies \neg p$**
**always::always/sometimes** true
